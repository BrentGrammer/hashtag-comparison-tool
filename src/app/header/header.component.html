Hashtag Comparison Tool

Find common hashtags used on highly viewed Social Media posts!

Suggested Use:
  1. Look up a hashtag or search a social media platform to get a list of posts.
  2. Copy and paste the hashtags from the posts with a high like count and paste into a Hashtag group per post.
  3. Click the Submit button to display a list of common hashtags in all the different groups entered.
  4. Use the list of hashtags to find long tail results (hashtags with enough hits, not too few or too many).
